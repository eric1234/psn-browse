Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain

The 5th installment of the Metal Gear Solid saga, Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain continues the story of Big Boss (aka Naked Snake, aka David), connecting the story lines from Metal Gear Solid: Peace Walker, Metal Gear Solid: Ground Zeroes, and the rest of the Metal Gear Universe.

Snake wakes up in a hospital after lying in a coma for 9 years only to find out that the hospital is under attack by an unknown enemy and must escape the hospital as quickly as possible - while only slowly regaining control over his body, degenerated by the coma.

About

A site for browsing the games available on PlayStation Now.

Previous options:

  • Official Site - Mostly text titles only. No search
  • App - Few broad category, poor search, limited game information
  • IGN - Decent category-based search. Poor other search options. Decent game info if a popular game.

The aim of this site is to be better. Titles are extracted from the official site on a daily basis so it is always up-to-date. Each title is then enriched with data for IGDB. Advanced faceted search to narrow down to the game you want. Open source so anybody can improve it.
