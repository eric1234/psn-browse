SOMA is a sci-fi horror game from Frictional Games, the creators of Amnesia: The Dark Descent. It is an unsettling story about identity, consciousness, and what it means to be human.

The radio has gone silent on PATHOS-2. As isolation bears down on the staff of the remote research facility, strange things are happening. Machines are taking on human traits and alien constructions have started to interfere with routine. The world around them is turning into a nightmare. The only way out is to do something unimaginable.

About

A site for browsing the games available on PlayStation Now.

Previous options:

  • Official Site - Mostly text titles only. No search
  • App - Few broad category, poor search, limited game information
  • IGN - Decent category-based search. Poor other search options. Decent game info if a popular game.

The aim of this site is to be better. Titles are extracted from the official site on a daily basis so it is always up-to-date. Each title is then enriched with data for IGDB. Advanced faceted search to narrow down to the game you want. Open source so anybody can improve it.
