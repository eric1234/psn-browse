Virtua Fighter 5: Final Showdown

The fifth entry in SEGA's advanced, realistic fighting game series. Once again, the realtime level of visual detail and character animation has been upped to the most advanced levels, with next-generation lighting and detail on the fighters and environments. Players will be able to customize their characters by selecting from various patterned costumes and a wide range of attachable items that can be placed onto each fighter's various body parts. Players will not only achieve victory by defeating highly-skilled opponents, but will also compete for prizes and earn in-game money through the match to buy many items at an in-game shop. Virtua Fighter 5: Final Showdown contains a full overhaul of Virtua Fighter 5's mechanics, balance and animations, along with new game modes, new dynamic fighting arenas, a massive collection of customizable character items and new characters.

About

A site for browsing the games available on PlayStation Now.

Previous options:

  • Official Site - Mostly text titles only. No search
  • App - Few broad category, poor search, limited game information
  • IGN - Decent category-based search. Poor other search options. Decent game info if a popular game.

The aim of this site is to be better. Titles are extracted from the official site on a daily basis so it is always up-to-date. Each title is then enriched with data for IGDB. Advanced faceted search to narrow down to the game you want. Open source so anybody can improve it.
