Sonic the Hedgehog 4: Episode II

The Sonic 4 Saga continues in Episode II with the return of a beloved side kick and fan-favorite villains! Following the events of Episode I, Metal Sonic has returned to form an alliance with Dr. Eggman! To face this new evil, Sonic will have to call upon an old friend to help him save the day!

After the events of Episode I, Little Planet returns, and Dr. Eggman locates and repairs Metal Sonic.[17] Sonic hears that Eggman is back, and reunites with Tails to stop him. Meanwhile, Metal Sonic searches for the heroes and sees them fly away in the Tornado, following them using Tails' rocket.

About

A site for browsing the games available on PlayStation Now.

Previous options:

  • Official Site - Mostly text titles only. No search
  • App - Few broad category, poor search, limited game information
  • IGN - Decent category-based search. Poor other search options. Decent game info if a popular game.

The aim of this site is to be better. Titles are extracted from the official site on a daily basis so it is always up-to-date. Each title is then enriched with data for IGDB. Advanced faceted search to narrow down to the game you want. Open source so anybody can improve it.
