The Vanishing of Ethan Carter

In The Vanishing of Ethan Carter, you play as Paul Prospero, an occult detective who receives a disturbing letter from Ethan Carter and realizes that the boy is in grave danger. When Paul arrives in Ethan's home of Red Creek Valley, he realizes things are even worse than he imagined. Ethan has vanished in the wake of a brutal murder, which Paul comes to see might not be the only murder to investigate. Using both regular and supernatural detective skills, Paul must figure out what happened to the missing boy.

About

A site for browsing the games available on PlayStation Now.

Previous options:

  • Official Site - Mostly text titles only. No search
  • App - Few broad category, poor search, limited game information
  • IGN - Decent category-based search. Poor other search options. Decent game info if a popular game.

The aim of this site is to be better. Titles are extracted from the official site on a daily basis so it is always up-to-date. Each title is then enriched with data for IGDB. Advanced faceted search to narrow down to the game you want. Open source so anybody can improve it.
